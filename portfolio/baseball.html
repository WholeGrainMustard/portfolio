Out of the park!

Alton Hipps

My style is called In the Ballpark and is a naturalistic take on a baseball park themed map.

I drew inspiration from classic ballparks, player uniforms, and baseballs themselves.

In the map, baseballs represent places of interest, and ballpark icons the 30 Major League Baseball ballparks.

The map uses textures from baseball with inspired fonts fill patterns.

Take a look at a few different geographies, starting with Madison, Wisconsin.
